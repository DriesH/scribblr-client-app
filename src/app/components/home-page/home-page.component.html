What is Scribblr?

Scribblr is the place where you can collect all your memories of your children. Got any funny quotes or amazing stories you never want to forget?
We'll take care of that.


Of course we know how much you still love the real thing. That's why we offer you the ability to print all these memories in a beautiful flip-over or pocket book.

Quotes and stories

Kids have amazing amounts of creativity. A lot more than adults. That's why children often say funny or ingenious things during their childhood.


Kids are also real explorers. As they grow up, they constantly learn and experience new things.

That one amazing day at the seaside, their first day of school or the day they make the most beautiful drawing.


All these memories are worth saving! With Scribblr this is only a matter of seconds.
